About Sai pallavi

Sai Pallavi Senthamarai Kannan (born 9 May 1992), known professionally as Sai Pallavi, is an Indian actress who works in Tamil, Telugu and Malayalam films.[1] She is the recipient of numerous accolades, including four Filmfare Awards South and two South Indian International Movie Awards. She was featured by Forbes magazine as one of India's 30 under 30 in 2020 Pallavi made her acting debut with the 2015 Malayalam film Premam, which was the second highest-grossing Malayalam film at that point.
